Case study

BI Agent

Conversational analytics that turns plain-English questions into accurate, explainable T-SQL over enterprise data.

Role AI Developer & Automation Engineer Org Al-Obeikan Digital Solutions Domain Analytics
Get in touch → All work →

The fastest query is the one you can ask in your own words.

The BI Agent turns natural-language questions into T-SQL over enterprise databases. A semantic layer and a knowledge graph map business terms to data models, so answers are accurate and explainable rather than plausible guesses.

Results do not stop at a raw table. They render through an integrated dashboard builder that turns a single question into an interactive, decision-ready view.
